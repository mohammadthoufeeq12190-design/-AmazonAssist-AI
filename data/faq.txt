Q: How can I track my order?
A: Go to My Orders and select Track Package.

Q: How long does a refund take?
A: Refunds usually take 5 to 7 business days.

Q: Can I cancel a shipped order?
A: Cancellation may not be available after shipment.

Q: What payment methods are supported?
A: UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking and COD.

Q: What are Prime benefits?
A: Prime members receive free delivery and exclusive offers.

Q: How do I return a product?
A: Open My Orders and select Return Item.

Q: Why did my payment fail?
A: Payment failures can occur due to OTP issues, network problems or insufficient balance.

Q: How do I apply a coupon?
A: Enter the coupon code during checkout.

Q: What happens if delivery is delayed?
A: You can track the latest delivery status from My Orders.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: Contact support via chat, email or phone.
